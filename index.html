About

is-hardly.online is place for perpetually sleepy internet users to tinker and interact with friends. Furries, hackers, and artists alike are welcome and can rest here to hold the eebies at bay.

Several services are hosted here, some with open account registration and other with application required. If there is a new service you'd like to see here, feel free to reach out to katlyn with your request.

Services

Listed below are the currently offered public services, along with any additional information about them.

mastodon.is-hardly.online

A Mastodon instance running Chuckya with an increased character limit (4096 chars). Account creation is open on the instance, with moderator approval required.

matrix.is-hardly.online

A Matrix homeserver powered by Synapse. Registrations are closed, but if desired accounts may be requested by contacting katlyn.
